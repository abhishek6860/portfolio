Discover a Hotel & Resort to Book a Suitable Room

Project Overview

Discover a Hotel & Resort is a user-friendly travel application designed to help travelers find and book suitable hotel rooms and resorts with ease. The platform provides a seamless booking experience, allowing users to browse accommodations, compare prices, check availability, and make reservations conveniently.


Timeframe

4 Weeks

My role

UX + UI Design, Visual design, Branding, User flow, Research, Prototyping + Testing

Tools

Figma, Fig jam, Zoom, Google Forms

The Challenge

Simplifying Hotel Booking – Fast, Easy, and Personalized for Every Traveller!

Travelers often struggle with finding and booking suitable hotels due to complex interfaces, inefficient search and filtering systems, and lengthy booking processes. Many platforms fail to provide personalized recommendations, leading to an overwhelming number of choices. Additionally, poor mobile optimization makes it difficult for users to book on the go. A lack of structured reviews and a seamless payment process further adds to user frustration, resulting in a high drop-off rate.

Objective/Goal

The goal of this travel application is to simplify the hotel booking experience by providing an intuitive, user-friendly, and visually appealing interface. It aims to streamline search, filtering, and booking flows, ensuring a fast and hassle-free process. The application will focus on mobile-first design, secure payments, and personalized recommendations to enhance user satisfaction. Additionally, it will offer a structured review system and an admin dashboard for hotel partners, ensuring a seamless experience for both travellers and hotel owners.

Future Impact

This project represents an important step in supporting the natural hair community with tools that empower users to embrace and manage their hair journeys. The following recommendations and potential future impacts could help guide the development and long-term growth of the app.

  1. Enhanced Personalization Through AI: As the app gathers more data, it can become more accurate in suggesting products, routines and services tailored to users' unique hair profiles. This will make it a go-to resource for highly customised hair care, fostering deeper user engagement.
  2. Partnerships with Hair Care Brands and Salons: Establish partnerships with local and international natural hair care brands and salons, allowing for product or service integrations directly in the app.
  3. Augmented Reality (AR) for Try-On Features: Incorporate AR features where users can virtually try on different hairstyles, cuts or hair colours to visualise what looks good on them before committing to changes. This could create an engaging and fun experience that makes the app more interactive, increasing user retention.
  4. Education and Advocacy: Introduce extensive educational content and resources about the history, politics and cultural significance of natural hair. This could take the form of blog posts, podcasts or mini-documentaries. By educating users on the deeper significance of natural hair, the app can build a stronger emotional connection with its audience, leading to higher retention rates and making it a platform that celebrates and advocates for natural hair pride.

These suggestions could solidify 'Kinks and Coils' as a leader in the natural haircare industry and expand its influence on a global scale.
